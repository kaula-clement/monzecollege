About Us

About Monze college of education

We are a privately run college of Education offering Primary diploma, Secondary diploma and Early Childhood diploma under both full time and distance education.

Our vibrant campus comes not only from the daily engagement of accomplished students and renowned faculty, but also from the extraordinary energy, talent and cultural diversity that defines Zambia.

At Monze College of Education, we are committed to providing top-quality education to our student teachers across all study areas, equipping them with the necessary skills to meet the ever-evolving needs of their future students.

As you explore our website, you will discover our unwavering commitment to academic excellence, our renowned faculty, and our student community brimming with exceptional talent. Whether you are a prospective student, a parent, or an education enthusiast, we invite you to join us on this journey of knowledge and growth.

Experience the vibrancy of Monze College, where educational excellence meets cultural diversity, and let us help you shape a future filled with endless possibilities.

We firmly believe that in order to achieve true quality education, it is essential to invest in the right resources. With a dedicated focus on material resources, personnel, and infrastructure, we strive to create an optimal learning environment within our institution. By addressing these areas, we ensure the effective implementation of our primary school teachers' diploma program, paving the way for future degree programs.

Our vision aligns closely with the nation's development plan, which aims to provide innovative and productive lifelong education accessible to all by 2013. Monze College actively contributes to this vision through various projects aimed at fostering an enabling environment for quality education. By fostering an atmosphere of excellence, we aim to produce graduate teachers who will serve our nation admirably by meeting the diverse learning needs of every child.
